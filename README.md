## Flareless Torches

![latest release](https://img.shields.io/github/v/release/taipan9129/flareless-torches)
![download count](https://img.shields.io/github/downloads/taipan9129/flareless-torches/total?label=total%20downloads)

Are you getting tired of Avgi torch flares and Korath afterburners drowning out zuckung's custom flares? Well then you're in luck because this simple plugin adds flareless variants for the following:
- Z-333 and Z666 (can be found anywhere fusion torches are sold)
- Korath afterburners (available in Laki Nemparu)
